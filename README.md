# Boost your AI productivity with MDify!

MDify effortlessly converts any web page into clean, formatted Markdown, perfect for feeding into Large Language Models (LLMs) like Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, and Perplexity.

## Why MDify?

### The Problem with Web Links
You might wonder, "Why not just paste the URL into ChatGPT?"
*   **Hallucinations:** LLMs often guess page content based on the URL rather than actually reading the real-time data.
*   **Access Denied:** AI bots cannot access pages behind logins, paywalls, or your local development server (localhost).
*   **Context Clutter:** Raw web pages are filled with ads, navigation menus, and scripts that waste your context tokens (money) and confuse the model.

### The Solution
MDify converts the **actual content** you are viewing into clean, dense Markdown.
*   **WYSIWYG:** If you can see it in your browser, MDify can convert itâ€”even behind auth.
*   **LLM Native:** Markdown is the most efficient language for LLMs to understand structure.
*   **Token Efficient:** Drastically reduces token usage by stripping away the noise.

## Key Features

*   **One-Click Conversion:** Instantly turn articles, documentation, and blog posts into Markdown.
*   **Direct AI Injection:** Seamlessly insert the converted content directly into the chat input of your favorite AI tools.
*   **Clean UI:** Minimalist floating bubble that stays out of your way until you need it.
*   **Overlay Management:**
    *   **Minimize:** Tuck the bubbles away to the side.
    *   **Close Options:** Hover over the bubble to see the Close (X) icon.
        *   **Close Once:** Hide for 10 minutes (great for screenshots).
        *   **Always for this site:** Add to exclusion list (can be managed in options).
*   **Smart Token Estimation:** Real-time token counting with visual safety indicators ensures your content fits perfectly within AI context windows.
*   **Clipboard Support:** Copy the Markdown to your clipboard with a single click.
*   **Privacy-First:** All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to our servers.
